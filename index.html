Cicada

  1. Cicadas are best known for their extraordinary, highly synchronized life cycles.
  2. Cicada climbs out of its molted skin on a leaf covered by other molted skins.
  3. Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.

panda

  3. Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
